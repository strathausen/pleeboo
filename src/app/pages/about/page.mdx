## Our Mission

Making community organizing simple, free, and accessible to everyone.

## Why We Built This

Have you ever tried to organize a neighborhood potluck using a spreadsheet? Or coordinated volunteers for a school fundraiser through endless email chains? We have, and it was painful.

That's why we created Pleeboo - a dead-simple pledge board that:

- **Works instantly** - No sign-ups, no downloads, no hassle
- **Shares easily** - One link is all you need
- **Updates in real-time** - Everyone sees the latest status
- **Costs nothing** - Free forever, no hidden fees

## How It Works

### 1. Create Your Board
Give your event a name and description. Our AI assistant can even suggest common items and volunteers you might need based on your event type.

### 2. Share the Link
Send one simple link to your community via email, text, or social media. That's it.

### 3. Watch It Fill Up
Community members can pledge items or volunteer for tasks with just their name. No accounts needed.
